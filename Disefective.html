Diseffective

  1. Addiction to Social Media
    Platforms designed to capture user attention lead to overuse.

  2. Easy Accessibility
    The internet's 24/7 availability makes it easy for individuals to spend excessive time online.

  3. Lack of Digital Literacy
    Many users are unaware of the best practices for efficient internet use, leading to misinformation and wasted time.

  4. Mental Health Issues
    Excessive screen time can result in anxiety, depression, and loneliness.

  5. Loss of Productivity
    Spending too much time on non-productive sites affects work or academic performance.

  6. Cyberbullying and Harassment
    Misuse of social media can lead to cyberbullying, affecting mental well-being.

  7. Spread of Misinformation
    Incorrect use of the internet often results in the spread of fake news and false information.

  8. Time Management
    Setting limits on internet usage can help in maintaining a balanced lifestyle.

  9. Digital Literacy Education
    Learning how to verify information and use the internet responsibly is essential.

  10. Use of Monitoring Tools
    Apps that track and limit screen time can assist users in avoiding internet overuse.
