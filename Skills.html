Skills

  1. Basic Computer Literacy
    The ability to use a computer efficiently, including operating systems, using applications, and understanding file management.Forms the foundation for all other internet-related skills, allowing users to navigate websites, manage data, and use the web effectively.

  2. Web Browsing
    The skill of navigating websites using a web browser, understanding how to search for information, and using various browser tools like bookmarks, history, and extensions.Helps users efficiently find relevant information on the internet, research topics, and stay updated with the latest trends.

  3. Search Engine Proficiency
    The ability to use search engines (e.g., Google, Bing) effectively by employing relevant keywords, advanced search options, and filters.Ensures quick and accurate access to needed information, saving time and improving productivity.

  4. Online Communication
    The ability to communicate over the internet using various platforms like emails, messaging apps, video calls, and social media.Essential for both personal and professional communication, enabling global connectivity and collaboration.

  5. Social Media Management
    The skill of using social media platforms for personal expression, networking, marketing, and engaging with others.Valuable for building relationships, sharing information, and promoting businesses or personal brands.

  6. Online Security Awareness
    Knowledge of potential online threats like viruses, phishing, and data breaches, along with strategies for protecting personal information.Critical for safeguarding sensitive data and avoiding fraud, identity theft, or unauthorized access.

  7. Digital Content Creation
    The ability to create and share digital content, such as text, images, videos, or podcasts, across different platforms.Important for self-expression, marketing, education, and entertainment in todayâ€™s digital age.

  8. Cloud Computing Skills
    Understanding how to store, manage, and access data using cloud services like Google Drive, Dropbox, or Microsoft OneDrive.Enhances productivity by providing easy access to files from anywhere, promoting collaboration, and reducing hardware dependency.

  9. E-commerce Knowledge
    The skill of buying, selling, and managing online transactions through websites and apps.Crucial for leveraging the online marketplace for business, shopping, or services, and understanding secure payment methods.

  10. Problem-Solving and Critical Thinking
    The ability to troubleshoot technical issues, evaluate the credibility of information, and find solutions to various challenges online.Helps users navigate the internet safely and efficiently, making informed decisions in a rapidly changing digital landscape.
